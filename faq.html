FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a loan?

Go to the Loans section, fill in your KYC details, select the loan amount and submit. Our team will verify and approve it.

How do I check my EMI due date?

Your next EMI due date is displayed on the Dashboard under the Next EMI section.

How do I update my bank account?

Go to Add Bank section in the dashboard and enter your new bank details. Make sure the account is Aadhaar-linked.

What if my loan payment fails?

If a payment fails, the admin will notify you. You can re-upload the correct transaction screenshot to complete payment.

How do I contact support?

Go to the Support section in More tab or email us at support@dhanifinance.com
